About This Project

23 Calle Bucare is an ongoing renovation at a private residence in Puerto Rico, and this proposal extends the scope of an existing engagement with Juliette Calaf Interiors. Having already collaborated on the kitchen project management, we are now moving into the private quarters of the home: the master bedroom, master bathroom, and master closet.

The architectural design for the master bathroom is already complete, with construction drawings produced by the project architect. Juliette Calaf Interiors will step in to direct the furnishing, finish selections, fixtures, and styling that bring the space to life. Project management during the construction phase will be handled on a time-and-materials basis at our standard hourly rates.

The master bedroom will be developed as a fully realized retreat, with attention to the complete furnishing and styling plan from the bed and textiles to lighting, art placement, and the final layer of curation that makes a room feel complete. The master closet will be designed and specified as a natural extension of the suite, with the same level of material quality and refinement applied throughout.

Together, these three spaces represent the heart of the private home. The result will be an environment designed to be lived in, returned to at the end of each day, and held for years to come.

Our Process & What Is Included
Our Process
1
Conceptual Development
Study of existing conditions, space planning, mood boards, floor plans, and design concepts. Construction timeline and aesthetic direction established across all three spaces.
2
Design Development
Specification of all finishes, fixtures, furnishings, window coverings, decorative lighting, and custom millwork direction. First design presentation held.
3
Procurement
Purchasing proposals issued for client approval. No item ordered without signed proposal and required payment. Vendor coordination and logistics managed by JCI.
4
Installation
Coordination of delivery, installation, and final styling. Two installation days included in the flat fee. Project management during construction billed separately at hourly rates.

Estimated timeline varies based on scope. Smaller or furnishing-only projects may be completed in significantly less time. Full-scope projects with custom fabrication typically run 18 to 24 months. A detailed project schedule will be provided at kickoff.*

*Timeline depends on scope, client approvals, vendor lead times, and construction schedule.

What Is Included
  • Full creative direction across all four service phases
  • Space planning, mood boards, floor plans, and ceiling plans
  • Finish, fixture, and FF&E selection and specification
  • Custom millwork direction and fabricator coordination
  • Two rounds of revisions per deliverable
  • Procurement management and vendor coordination
  • FF&E logistics and storage coordination
  • Two installation days included in the flat fee
  • Final styling and accessory curation
  • Six site visits included across Phases 1 through 4
Not Included
Permit applications, structural or engineering services, renderings (available as an additional service), revisions beyond two rounds per phase. Construction administration is billed separately at hourly rates per the Service Agreement dated March 31, 2026.
